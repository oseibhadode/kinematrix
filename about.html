A B O U T

First, Kinematrix is a fusion of kinesis and matrix. Its not just some fancy name I came up with. I want to design things that have life, things that can move you, get your attention. That’s the kinetic part of the equation. A matrix, mathematically, is an array of data in a two-dimensional or three-dimensional format. Designs should have information embedded in them with which the designs can communicate with anyone that sees it! There you go, a design that gets your attention and passes information to you is basically a ‘Kinematric’ design.


I’ve been in the design world, well not too long in my estimate. I am most passionate about giving an entity an identity through a logo. Whether it be an organisation, a company, personal brand. I like people seeing something through a design. Design is the spice of life! And for that reason, I have found myself design anything I think I can design but I never forget to pass a message through my designs.


I have been privileged get involved with several projects, I look forward to working on bigger projects. Though big or small, I’m your guy still, I got your back!


Hope you had a nice time looking at the stuff I have in this portfolio website.


Thanks for your time.


Oh! and by the way, my name is Oseikhuemen Ibhadode.
